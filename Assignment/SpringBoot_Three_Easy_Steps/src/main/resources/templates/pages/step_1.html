Step 1



Cut and Skewer the Steaks:

Cut the roast across the grain into three steaks, each one roughly three inches across.

(The grain runs from the “tip” to the wide end of the roast.)

Skewer the steaks with the spit, bending them into a “C” shape with the fat cap on top (the outside of the “C” – again).

Watch your fingers!

Salt the steaks heavily, then let them rest at room temperature while you preheat the grill.
