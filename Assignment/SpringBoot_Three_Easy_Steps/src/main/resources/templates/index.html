PICANHA – ROTISSERIE ON THE CHARCOAL GRILL



Picanha is a Top Sirloin Cap Roast, with the fat still on (the fat is key).

It is cut across the grain into three steaks, bent into a “C” shape, and cooked on the rotisserie.

This is tutorial with a three easy steps how to make Picanha rotisserie on the charcoal grill.
